Student Management

Complete lifecycle management from admission to alumni

Status: Implemented

Key Features

Admission Process

Online application form, document upload, application tracking, and merit list publication.

Registration

Course registration, fee payment integration, and timetable generation.

Academic Records

Maintain student academic history, grades, credits, and transcripts.

ID Card Management

Generate and manage student identity cards with QR codes for verification.

User Guide

For Students

To access the student portal:

  1. Visit cit.samarth.ac.in
  2. Click on the "Student Login" button
  3. Enter your enrollment number and password
  4. After logging in, you can access your dashboard

Course Registration

During the registration period:

  1. Go to the "Academics" section
  2. Select "Course Registration"
  3. Choose your preferred courses from the available options
  4. Submit your selection and pay the required fees
  5. Download and print the registration confirmation

For Administrators

To manage student records:

  1. Login with your admin credentials
  2. Navigate to the "Student Management" section
  3. Use the search filters to find specific students
  4. Click on a student to view or edit their details
  5. Use the bulk operations for batch updates

Note: Student data modifications require approval from the department head. Always verify changes with the student's academic coordinator.

HR Management

Complete human resource management for staff and faculty

Status: Implemented

Key Features

Employee Records

Complete database of all employees with personal and professional details.

Payroll Management

Salary processing, deductions, bonuses, and reimbursement management.

Leave Management

Apply for leave, track balances, and approval workflows.

Performance Management

Appraisal system, goal setting, and performance reviews.

User Guide

For Employees

To access your HR profile:

  1. Visit cit.samarth.ac.in
  2. Click on the "Employee Login" button
  3. Enter your employee ID and password
  4. Access your dashboard with personal and professional information

Applying for Leave

To submit a leave request:

  1. Go to the "Leave Management" section
  2. Click on "Apply for Leave"
  3. Select leave type, dates, and provide a reason
  4. Submit the application for approval
  5. Track the status in your leave dashboard

For HR Administrators

To manage employee records:

  1. Login with HR admin credentials
  2. Navigate to the "Employee Management" section
  3. Use filters to find specific employees
  4. Manage employee details, roles, and permissions
  5. Generate reports for management review

Note: HR data is sensitive and confidential. Ensure proper authorization before accessing or modifying employee records. Always follow institutional data privacy policies.

Remarks & Roles

User Roles

Students

All enrolled students can access their profiles, register for courses, view grades, and update personal information.

Faculty Advisors

Can view advisee records, approve course registrations, and monitor academic progress.

Department Administrators

Manage student records, process applications, generate reports, and oversee department-level academic operations.

System Administrators

Full access to configure system settings, manage user permissions, and troubleshoot issues.

This module was implemented in January 2023. Integration with the examination module is scheduled for Q4 2023.
